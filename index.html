Our technology stack

We use up-to-date technologies and avoid starting from zero. We often furnish to the existing open-source projects.

Backend

Node.js (MeanStack), PHP, Symfony, Google Cloud Pub

We use for most of our client projects Symfony framework.

CR8TE can ensure your back-end integrates seamlessly with your entire ecosystem—ERP, CRM, CMS, or anything in between.

For selected projects we also use Node.js as the backend. Google Cloud Pub is our current choice for asynchronous data and messaging processing.

Data

MySQL, Apache Solr,GraphQL

CR8TE creates intelligent, reliable digital tools for accessing and managing relational databases. It’s all powered by our expert MySQL development services.

We also work a lot with Solr as a search, document database and data aggregation engine.

We use GraphQL as main query language for our API.

Frontend

Frontend

Vultr, Azure, Gitlab, Embold, Chef, Docker
